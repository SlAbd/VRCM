Climbing in the Todra Gorge

Experience the thrill of vertical rock climbing on the stunning limestone walls of Morocco's most spectacular gorge. Expert guides, breathtaking views, and unforgettable adventures await.

We recommend booking at least 1 week in advance

Ait Abdellah Abdelhadi

Hadi is a passionate local guide with over 13 years of experience in rock climbing and mountain adventures in the Todra Gorge region. Born and raised in the heart of the Atlas Mountains, he brings authentic knowledge of the terrain, culture, and the best climbing routes.

With Hadi, you'll discover hidden gems, learn traditional Berber hospitality, and experience Morocco's natural beauty in the safest and most memorable way possible.
